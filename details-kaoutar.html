About Me

This project was created as part of my Master's program, showcasing our class of 2024-2025. But in this part, I'll be talking about myself:

I am currently pursuing a Master's 2 in Computer Science at the University of Corsica, specialising in web and mobile development. In addition, I hold a Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Algiers 1 in Algeria, which has provided me with a deep understanding of AI concepts and allowed me to gain significant hands-on experience in this rapidly evolving field.

Throughout my academic journey, I have had the privilege of working on various projects that have strengthened my technical expertise. Notably, I contributed to the development of a recommendation system for scientific articles during my work placement at the Research Centre for Scientific and Technical Information in Algeria. Additionally, I was involved in the creation of multiple web applications, including an appointment management platform for a blood transfusion centre and a thesis recommendation website using artificial intelligence technologies.

These experiences have helped me to refine my skills in key technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React, Vue.js, and Node.js. Moreover, I have become proficient in database management using MySQL and MongoDB. With a solid foundation in both web development and artificial intelligence, I am eager to continue expanding my knowledge and making meaningful contributions in the tech industry.
